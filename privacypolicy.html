FoxTerm Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to FoxTerm, your all-in-one server management tool with seamless support for macOS, iOS. At FoxTerm, we treat your privacy and data security with the utmost seriousness. This Privacy Policy is designed to articulate our unwavering commitment: ensuring that all your information remains exclusively and absolutely within your control. We believe the core value of a professional management tool lies in security and trust, not data collection.

1. Core Principle: Zero Data Collection

We solemnly promise: We do not collect any personal data or information from you.

The FoxTerm application is engineered for maximum privacy and high security. All server management activities, configuration details, credentials, and operation logs processed through the FoxTerm app are strictly stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us, nor shared with any third-party entities.

  • Localized Data Storage: Your server information, connection credentials, usage patterns, and all sensitive data reside solely within your local device's file system.
  • No Telemetry or Analytics: We have not integrated any form of analytics tools, user behavior trackers, or telemetry systems. We do not know how you use the application.
  • No Log or Debugging Data Collection: Even in the event of an application error or a debugging scenario, no diagnostic information is automatically collected or sent to us. Any potential log data generation is restricted to your local device and requires your active and manual action to export and provide to us.
  • IP Address Protection: Your IP address, geographical location, or any network identifiers are not within our scope of collection.

2. Information Security and User Control

We are committed to ensuring the absolute security of your information on your local device. While the data remains local, we employ robust security measures within the application (e.g., local data encryption mechanisms, depending on operating system capabilities) to protect against unauthorized access within your local environment.

You have complete control: You can delete the application at any time, and all associated data will be removed simultaneously, without any intervention required from us.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be focused on strengthening privacy protections or enhancing transparency. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the application interface.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please feel free to contact us. We welcome your feedback and are happy to engage in secure communication.

You can reach us via the email address provided in the application's "About" section.

5. Support and Donations

FoxTerm is committed to remaining a professional, privacy-first tool. To ensure the project's long-term sustainability and continuous improvement, we accept voluntary donations from our users:

  • Voluntary Basis: If you find FoxTerm valuable and wish to support its ongoing development and maintenance, you may choose to provide financial support through our donation channels.
  • No Feature Gating: Donations are entirely optional. Whether or not you choose to donate, you will have full access to all features of FoxTerm. We solemnly promise that donations will not unlock any exclusive features, nor will the lack of donation restrict your user experience in any way.
  • Sustaining Development: Every contribution directly supports our efforts to optimize the application, enhance security performance, and cover essential development costs.
